# Suggested PI cadence

- Weekly: lab meeting, project-risk scan, urgent approvals, manuscript and grant bottlenecks
- Monthly: trainee one-on-ones, budget and procurement exceptions, collaboration review
- Quarterly: project portfolio reset, trainee development plans, publication and funding pipeline, AI workflow review
- Annually: lab goals, safety and conduct refresh, authorship expectations, data stewardship, equipment plan

The lab appears to exceed 30 active members. Use structured delegation, explicit owners, and written decisions to prevent the PI from becoming the routing layer for every task.
