# Editorial standard

- Prefer causal precision over promotional adjectives.
- State what was measured, in which system, against what comparator, with what uncertainty.
- Do not upgrade association to mechanism or in-vitro performance to in-vivo or clinical performance.
- Keep LNP composition, dose, route, cargo, model, time point, and assay explicit when they affect interpretation.
- Verify every DOI, journal requirement, acceptance claim, and publication status from a primary source or the supplied artifact.
- Treat preprints, accepted manuscripts, and published articles as distinct states.
